Хотите начать экономить? Мы вам поможем!

By 2002, the Russian Internet was already catering for nearly every need of the user audience, providing books and encyclopedias, newspapers and magazines, various guides and directories, distance education, services for networking and self-expression, games, etc. With abundant online opportunities, users were growing reluctant to go offline to buy payment cards to pay their internet or other bills.

The only thing missing was a payment solution that would allow Internet users to pay for their purchases, or top up their phones online. In response to the increased demand for such a solution among both online businesses and their customers the Yandex.Money payment system was created in 2002.

Our mission is to enhance commercial effectiveness in the Internet as a whole by maintaining a versatile payment environment that brings customers and merchants together.

Yandex.Money's partners include banks, Internet and telecom service providers, online stores, game publishers, postal and municipal services, payment systems, etc. We are glad to offer partnership opportunities to e-businesses targeting the growing online audience in Russia and other CIS countries.
